Learn Japanese App

Have you ever wanted to learn Japanese? Now you can! There are two quizzes to help you learn both of the phonetic alphabets. The quizzes will increment your overall score if you get a correct answer. Your highest streak will be counted. However, if you get an incorrect answer, the highest streak will remain but the current streak will reset back to zero. Below the input field will show the correct answer. Enjoy!
